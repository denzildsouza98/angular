Instructions to give the Quiz

  • You can get the questions by clicking on the Get Questions button on the menu bar
  • To submit your answers you can click the Submit Answers button on the menu bar
  • You then have to enter the question number and option number of the answer and press the ADD/Update answer button
  • The list of answers that you have submitted will be displayed below the submit option
  • Once you submit a answer you would have to refresh the screen for the answer or updated answer to appear
  • There isnt any limitation on how many times you can update your answer
  • To get the result you would have to click on the Get Result button in the menu bar
  • You can review the correct answers ans you answers by clicking review correct answers
  • You can give the test any number of times that you need

Note:Every time you go click the get result button a new attempt is added

Marking scheme

  • Each correct answer will add 5 marks to your score
  • Each wrong answer will decrease 1 mark from your score
  • No marks will be added or reduced for questions that are not answered
  • You cannot unmark an answer if you wish to not attempt it you can update the question putting 0 as the answer number
